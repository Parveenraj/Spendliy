Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account with Spendly, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. When you use the Service, we also collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

We do not collect sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers, card details, or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly experience. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Display your expenses and spending summaries within the app
  • Authenticate your account securely
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the Service

We do not use your data for advertising purposes, and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, or deletion.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use trusted third-party services to help operate the platform — for example, hosting providers. These parties have access to your data only to the extent necessary to perform their functions, and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

We do not integrate with third-party advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other websites.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses only essential cookies necessary for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. By using the Service, you consent to the use of these essential cookies.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact information available on the site. We will respond to all reasonable requests within a reasonable timeframe.
